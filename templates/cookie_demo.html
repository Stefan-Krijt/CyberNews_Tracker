HttpOnly Cookie Protection Demo

What are HttpOnly Cookies?

HttpOnly cookies cannot be accessed by JavaScript, protecting them from Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) attacks.

Instructions:

  1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to the Console tab
  3. Type document.cookie and press Enter
  4. Observe which cookie appears!
